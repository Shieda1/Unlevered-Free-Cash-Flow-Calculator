What is the unlevered free cash flow calculator?
The unlevered free cash flow calculator is a tool that determines the free cash available to the equity holders and debt holders.
How to calculate unlevered free cash flow?
To calculate unlevered free cash flow you should use the following formula:
unlevered free cash flow = EBIT * (1 - effective tax rate) + depreciation and amortization - capital expenditures + net change in working capital
